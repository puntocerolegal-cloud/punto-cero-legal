## LAYER DEPENDENCIES

```
LAYER 1: VISION
  Dependencies: None
  Impact on: Everything
  Stability: Permanent

LAYER 2: CONSTITUTION
  Dependencies: Vision
  Impact on: All systems
  Stability: Permanent (evolves by amendment)

LAYER 3: GOVERNANCE
  Dependencies: Vision, Constitution
  Impact on: All decisions
  Stability: Permanent (evolves formally)

LAYER 4: BUSINESS RULES
  Dependencies: Vision, Constitution, Governance
  Impact on: Operations
  Stability: Stable (changes through governance)

LAYER 5: KNOWLEDGE
  Dependencies: Vision, Constitution, Business Rules
  Impact on: Intelligence, Services
  Stability: Growing (added continuously)

LAYER 6: DARWIN INTELLIGENCE
  Dependencies: Vision, Constitution, Knowledge, Business Rules
  Impact on: Services, Channels
  Stability: Continuously improved

LAYER 7: BUSINESS SERVICES
  Dependencies: All previous layers
  Impact on: Applications, Channels
  Stability: Operational

LAYER 8: APPLICATIONS
  Dependencies: All previous layers
  Impact on: User experience
  Stability: Regularly updated

LAYER 9: CHANNELS
  Dependencies: All service layers
  Impact on: Customer engagement
  Stability: Continuously expanded

LAYER 10: INFRASTRUCTURE
  Dependencies: All previous layers
  Impact on: System capability
  Stability: Replaceable
```
