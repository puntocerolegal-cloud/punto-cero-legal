## DESIGN PRINCIPLES

### 1. **Executive Authority**
- Darwin has authority to make business decisions
- Not asking permission, making decisions
- Based on established policies and rules
- Transparent reasoning for all decisions

### 2. **Complete Context**
- Every decision has full context
- Customer, market, vertical, temporal context
- Historical patterns considered
- Future projections included

### 3. **Zero External Dependency**
- Not dependent on OpenAI
- Not dependent on Google
- Not dependent on Anthropic
- Pure Punto Cero intellectual property

### 4. **Multi-Dimensional Operations**
- Multi-país (country-aware)
- Multi-moneda (currency-aware)
- Multi-empresa (tenant-isolated)
- Multi-canal (channel-specific)
- Multi-vertical (industry-specific)

### 5. **Quality Assurance**
- Every response audited
- Every decision justified
- Every action documented
- Every outcome measured

### 6. **Continuous Learning**
- Records what works
- Records what doesn't work
- Identifies patterns
- Improves over time
- NO external ML, pure architectural learning

### 7. **No Production Changes**
- Pure orchestration layer
- Zero modifications to existing systems
- Completely additive
- Can be disabled without impact
