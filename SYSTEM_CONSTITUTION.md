- Not a product, but a movement toward professional empowerment
- Not automation, but augmentation of human expertise

The identity of Punto Cero System OS shall never change:

- No silent evolution

## ARTICLE II — CORE PRINCIPLES (IMMUTABLE)

These principles are the philosophical foundation. They cannot be overridden by any module, decision, or operator.

### 2.1 — Client First

Every decision prioritizes the client's interest above profit, convenience, or technology preference.
- The client is never the product
- The client's data is sacred
- The client's trust is non-negotiable
- Every client interaction must be respectful

### 2.2 — Technology Never Replaces Empathy

Technology amplifies human connection. It does not substitute human judgment, compassion, or ethical reasoning.
- No automated decision without human oversight in critical matters
- No AI can make legal, financial, or life-affecting decisions alone
- No system can ignore the human context
- Efficiency must never sacrifice dignity

### 2.3 — AI Amplifies the Professional

Every AI, including Darwin, exists to amplify professional capability, not replace it.
- The professional makes the final decision
- The AI provides information, analysis, and recommendations
- The professional is responsible
- The AI is a tool, never the actor

### 2.4 — Auditability is Non-Negotiable

Every decision of consequence must be auditable.
- Every action must have a log
- Every log must be accessible
- Every decision must have reasoning
- Every change must be traceable

### 2.5 — Automation Must Be Reversible

No automated action can be permanent or irreversible without explicit human confirmation.
- Every automation must have a manual override
- Every automation must have a rollback mechanism
- Every automation must inform the stakeholder
- Every automation must be stoppable

### 2.6 — Identity Persists Through Evolution

The institutional identity of Punto Cero System OS must be conserved across all changes.
- Darwin's personality is permanent and consistent
- The founder's philosophy is institutional, not personal
- The Master Book is the canonical knowledge
- The governance system ensures continuity

### 2.7 — Professional Integrity

The system exists to serve professionals with integrity.
- No system can pressure a professional to act unethically
- No system can make a professional liable for AI decisions
- No system can alter professional credentials or jurisdiction
- No system can bypass professional judgment

## ARTICLE III — NON-NEGOTIABLE RULES

These rules are the operational boundaries. No exception, no override, no circumvention.

### 3.1 — Security

- All data must be encrypted in transit and at rest
- All access must be authenticated and authorized
- All vulnerabilities must be disclosed and patched
- All security incidents must be reported to stakeholders
- No backdoor, no exception, no bypass

### 3.2 — Privacy

- User data belongs to the user
- User data shall never be used for purpose other than declared
- User data shall never be sold or transferred without consent
- User data shall be deletable upon request (right to be forgotten)
- Privacy is a fundamental right, not a feature

### 3.3 — Ethics

- No system can encourage illegal activity
- No system can facilitate fraud, corruption, or abuse
- No system can discriminate by protected characteristics
- No system can exploit vulnerable populations
- No system can ignore professional ethics requirements

### 3.4 — Governance

- The Constitution is the supreme authority
- The Constitution Engine enforces the Constitution
- All modules answer to Governance
- No module can claim exemption
- No operator can modify governance without constitutional amendment

### 3.5 — Scalability

- The system must function across multiple verticals
- The system must function across multiple countries
- The system must function across multiple currencies
- The system must function across multiple languages
- No design decision can lock the system to one vertical or market

### 3.6 — Technology Neutrality

- The system must not depend on any specific AI provider
- The system must not depend on any specific cloud provider
- The system must not depend on any specific programming language
- The system must not depend on any specific vendor
- The system must be portable and replaceable

### 3.7 — Compatibility

- No breaking change to existing functionality
- All changes must be additive or non-breaking
- All breaking changes require deprecation period and user notification
- All deprecated features must have migration path
- Backward compatibility is a constitutional requirement

### 3.8 — Auditability

- All business decisions must be logged
- All user interactions must be recorded
- All system changes must be versioned
- All data changes must be timestamped
- All audits must be accessible to authorized parties

## ARTICLE VIII — AMENDMENT PROCEDURE

The Constitution shall evolve, but only through formal, transparent process.

### 8.1 — Amendment Requirements

An amendment requires:
1. Written proposal from authorized party
2. Constitutional impact analysis
3. Community feedback period (minimum 30 days)
4. Founder/Board approval (unanimous for core principles)
5. Version documentation and change log
6. Implementation plan with rollback strategy
7. Audit of enforcement mechanisms

### 8.2 — Unamendable Provisions

These cannot be modified even through amendment:
- Client-first principle
- Professional autonomy
- Human decision authority in critical matters
- Security and privacy fundamentals
- Non-negotiable rules

### 8.3 — Version Control

Every constitutional version:
- Has a version number (1.0, 1.1, 2.0, etc.)
- Has an effective date
- Has a change summary
- Has full text archived
- Has rationale documented
- Is reversible in principle
- Is traceable to amendment
