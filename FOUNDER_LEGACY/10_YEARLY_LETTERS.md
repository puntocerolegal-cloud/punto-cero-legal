**Access:** All Team Members, Future AI Systems  

---

## WHY YEARLY LETTERS EXIST

Once a year, the founder pauses to reflect on the full year and share that perspective with the organization.

These letters become:
- Historical records of how Punto Cero evolved
- The founder's annual perspective on growth
- Guidance for the year ahead
- Connection between founder and team
- Training material showing how the founder thinks over time

---

## HOW YEARLY LETTERS WORK

Each letter covers one calendar year:
- What happened in the year
- What we learned
- How the company evolved
- What changed in the founder's thinking
- What's ahead

Letters should be personal, honest, and substantial—not a business report, but a genuine reflection.

## GUIDELINES FOR FOUNDER

**Timing:**
- Write at the very end of the year (December)
- Take time to reflect
- Write when you can be honest and thoughtful
- Don't rush it; this is important

**Tone:**
- Personal and genuine
- Honest about challenges
- Grateful for team
- Hopeful about future
- Vulnerable about growth

**Length:**
- As long as needed
- 2,000-5,000 words typical
- Better too long than too short
- Substance matters more than brevity

**Content:**
- Real numbers (good and bad)
- Real stories from the year
- Real learnings and insights
- Real acknowledgment of struggles
- Real vision for what's ahead

**Frequency:**
- Once per year, at year-end
- Same time each year creates a rhythm
- Future teams will look forward to these
