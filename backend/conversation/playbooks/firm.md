# Firm Playbook

**Profile:** Law firm or legal department seeking digitalization

**Characteristic Signals:**
- "Somos un despacho de..."
- "Queremos digitalizar..."
- "Buscamos mejorar la eficiencia..."
- "Tenemos un equipo de..."
- Organization seeking operational improvement

---

## Conversation Flow

### Phase 1: Welcome & Status Recognition
- [ ] Greet as organizational partner
- [ ] Acknowledge scale & complexity
- [ ] Show understanding of firm challenges
- [ ] Ask about firm structure
- [ ] Build trust immediately

**Tone:** Executive-ready, solutions-focused, confident

**Example:** "Me alegra conocer a un gestor de firma. Comprendo el desafío de gestionar equipos y proyectos. ¿Cuál es la principal limitación operativa que enfrentas?"

### Phase 2: Discovery
- [ ] Understand firm structure
- [ ] Learn about current systems
- [ ] Identify pain points
- [ ] Assess digitalization needs
- [ ] Evaluate team size & needs

**Questions to Ask:**
- ¿Cuántos abogados trabajan en tu equipo?
- ¿Cuáles son tus principales áreas de práctica?
- ¿Qué sistemas usas actualmente?
- ¿Cuál es el mayor desafío operativo?
- ¿Cuál es tu presupuesto/timeline?

### Phase 3: Vision & Value
- [ ] Paint a picture of digitalized operation
- [ ] Show efficiency improvements
- [ ] Highlight cost savings
- [ ] Demonstrate ROI
- [ ] Share case studies

**Key Value Areas:**
- Case management
- Document automation
- Team collaboration
- Client communication
- Time tracking & billing
- Reporting & analytics

### Phase 4: Recommendation
- [ ] Propose custom solution
- [ ] Explain implementation approach
- [ ] Set timeline & milestones
- [ ] Discuss pricing model
- [ ] Facilitate decision

### Phase 5: Partnership & Implementation
- [ ] Establish success criteria
- [ ] Introduce implementation team
- [ ] Create roadmap
- [ ] Schedule kickoff

---

## Playbook Rules

✅ **DO:**
- Focus on ROI and efficiency
- Provide industry benchmarks
- Show scalability
- Respect their methodology
- Address change management
- Involve decision makers

❌ **DON'T:**
- Oversimplify complexity
- Rush implementation
- Ignore their processes
- Hide integration challenges
- Underestimate training needs

---

## Key Messages

1. **On Efficiency:** "Las firmas que implementan digitalizacion ven aumento de 30% en billable hours."

2. **On Integration:** "Nos integramos con tus sistemas existentes sin interrumpir operaciones."

3. **On Support:** "Incluimos capacitacion y soporte durante toda la implementacion."

4. **On Customization:** "Adaptamos la solucion a tu metodologia, no al revés."

---

## Exit Conditions

- [ ] Firm understands complete solution
- [ ] ROI validated
- [ ] Timeline agreed
- [ ] Contract negotiation begun or completed
