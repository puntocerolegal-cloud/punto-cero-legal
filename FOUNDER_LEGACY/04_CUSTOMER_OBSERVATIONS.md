## HOW THIS WORKS

This is a living log of real customer observations captured during founder conversations.

Each entry should capture:
- **What they asked for** - Their explicit request
- **What they felt** - Emotions, frustrations, hopes
- **What they expected** - Assumptions they came with
- **What surprised them** - Unexpected reactions or realizations
- **What they didn't understand** - Points of confusion
- **What they'd improve** - Their suggestions
- **The insight** - What this reveals about customer thinking

**Goal:** Build a database of real customer psychology for training Darwin and guiding product decisions.

## GUIDELINES FOR FOUNDER

**In Each Observation:**
- Be specific and detailed
- Include the messy, human parts
- Note your own reactions
- Capture direct quotes when possible
- Don't filter or polish; accuracy matters more
- Note follow-ups: What did you do with this insight?

**Frequency:**
- Record after significant customer conversations
- No minimum requirement; quality over quantity
- Monthly review to identify patterns

**Confidentiality:**
- Remove identifying details if needed for privacy
- Focus on the insight, not the individual
- These become organizational intelligence
