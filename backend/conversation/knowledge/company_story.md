When Punto Cero expands to new markets:
- Company story remains the SAME (core identity)
- Products change (different verticals)
- Problems may differ (but root philosophy applies)
- Language may differ (but principles remain)

- Vertical 1 (Legal): "We solve legal access problems"
- Vertical 2 (Accounting): "We solve accounting efficiency problems"
- Vertical 3 (HR): "We solve HR management problems"
- **Core story:** "We democratize professional services"
