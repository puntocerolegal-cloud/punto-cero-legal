## HOW THIS WORKS

This is a living repository of ideas. Not all will be built. Not all are fully formed.

Some might seem impossible today but become obvious in 5 years.
Some will turn out to be problems we don't want to solve.
Some will be the foundation of our biggest future products.

This log preserves founder thinking so future leaders can understand:
- What the vision was
- What opportunities we saw
- What problems we thought mattered
- What scale we were imagining
