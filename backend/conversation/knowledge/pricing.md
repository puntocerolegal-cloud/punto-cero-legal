# Pricing Knowledge Base

**Purpose:** Pricing structure, ROI calculation, cost justification

## Pricing Principles

### Transparency First
- No hidden fees
- Clear breakdown of costs
- Money-back guarantee
- Flexible cancellation

### Value-Based Pricing
- Price reflects value delivered
- ROI is clear and measurable
- Compared to alternatives (especially DIY)
- Worth the investment

### Flexibility
- Multiple price points
- Payment plans available
- Volume discounts
- Custom enterprise pricing
