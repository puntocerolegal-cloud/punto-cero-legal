## WHY THIS LOG EXISTS

Mistakes are the best teachers, but only if we remember them.

This log ensures:
- Mistakes are never forgotten
- Lessons are preserved permanently
- Patterns in failures are identified
- Solutions are documented
- The same mistake doesn't happen twice

**Core principle:** Nothing in this log gets deleted. It's a permanent institutional memory of what we learned the hard way.

## GUIDELINES FOR FOUNDER

**Recording Mistakes:**
- Be completely honest
- Include your own role in the mistake
- Don't soften or excuse it
- Include the full impact
- Document the solution thoroughly

**Frequency:**
- Record significant mistakes within a week of discovery
- Never delete entries, even if embarrassing
- Add learnings and outcomes over time
- Review quarterly to identify patterns

**Sensitivity:**
- Anonymize customer/individual names if needed
- Focus on the lesson, not blame
- This is for learning, not punishment
- Share lessons, learn collectively

**Evolution:**
- As you solve similar problems better, document the improvement
- Link related mistakes and lessons
- Show how understanding grew over time
- Build a map of organizational learning
