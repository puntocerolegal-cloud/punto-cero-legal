## WHY THIS LOG EXISTS

Many decisions will be forgotten. Months or years later, someone will ask: "Why did we choose X instead of Y?" or "What was the thinking behind this?"

This log preserves:
- The problem that required decision
- The alternatives considered
- The chosen path
- The reasoning
- The expected outcomes
- The actual outcomes
- What was learned

## GUIDELINES FOR FOUNDER

**Recording a Decision:**
- Capture as close to the decision date as possible
- Include your reasoning, not just the choice
- Be honest about uncertainty
- Note what surprised you in the outcome
- Don't wait for perfect information on outcomes

**Frequency:**
- Major decisions: record immediately
- Routine decisions: batch monthly
- Crisis decisions: record after calm returns

**Revision:**
- Never change what was decided
- Add outcomes when they become clear
- Add learnings over time
- Build the narrative of actual impact
