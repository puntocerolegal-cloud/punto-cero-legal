## WHY THIS JOURNAL EXISTS

The best learning happens in small, regular moments.

A weekly reflection captures:
- What worked this week
- What didn't work
- What surprised us
- What we're learning in real-time
- What we need to change

This creates a living record of the founder's thinking evolution.

## GUIDELINES FOR FOUNDER

**Regular Reflection:**
- Ideally, reflect weekly (same day/time each week)
- 15-30 minutes of honest reflection
- Write naturally, not polished
- Include the uncertain and messy parts
- Don't wait for perfect understanding

**Frequency:**
- Weekly: Regular entries (15-30 min)
- Monthly: Synthesis of weekly themes (30 min)
- Quarterly: Bigger picture view (45 min)
- Annually: Full year reflection (1-2 hours)

**Honesty:**
- Write for yourself first, others second
- Include doubts and uncertainties
- Note what you got wrong
- Show how you're learning and changing
- This is the actual journey, not a polished narrative

**Evolution:**
- Your entries will show you growing
- Ideas will evolve through the year
- You'll see your own learning
- Future teams will see real leadership in action
- Darwin will learn how to actually think
