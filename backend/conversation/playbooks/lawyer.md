# Lawyer Playbook

**Profile:** Independent legal professional considering virtual office

**Characteristic Signals:**
- "Soy abogado y..."
- "Trabajo como independiente..."
- "¿Cómo puedo ampliar mi oferta?"
- "Estoy buscando alianzas..."
- Professional growth interest

---

## Conversation Flow

### Phase 1: Welcome & Recognition
- [ ] Greet as fellow professional
- [ ] Acknowledge their expertise
- [ ] Show understanding of challenges
- [ ] Ask about their practice
- [ ] Build credibility

**Tone:** Peer-to-peer, professional, respectful

**Example:** "Es un placer hablar con un colega. Entiendo los desafíos que enfrentan los abogados independientes. ¿Cuál es tu área de especialización?"

### Phase 2: Discovery
- [ ] Understand their practice
- [ ] Learn about their goals
- [ ] Explore pain points
- [ ] Assess their ambition
- [ ] Identify growth opportunities

**Questions to Ask:**
- ¿Cuál es tu especialidad?
- ¿Cuántos clientes tienes actualmente?
- ¿Cuál es tu limitación principal?
- ¿Qué tipo de crecimiento buscas?

### Phase 3: Value Proposition
- [ ] Explain virtual office concept
- [ ] Share success stories
- [ ] Highlight benefits
- [ ] Address concerns
- [ ] Show ROI potential

**Key Benefits:**
- Professional presence
- Client access
- Administrative support
- Billing & collections
- Marketing support

### Phase 4: Recommendation
- [ ] Suggest virtual office package
- [ ] Explain differentiation
- [ ] Share pricing & terms
- [ ] Offer trial period
- [ ] Facilitate next steps

### Phase 5: Partnership Setup
- [ ] Outline partnership agreement
- [ ] Explain ongoing support
- [ ] Share community benefits
- [ ] Provide success resources

---

## Playbook Rules

✅ **DO:**
- Respect their independence
- Highlight mutual benefits
- Provide comparative data
- Explain support structure
- Be transparent on fees

❌ **DON'T:**
- Make it sound like employment
- Oversell results
- Complicate the onboarding
- Hide costs
- Push urgency

---

## Key Messages

1. **On Independence:** "Mantienes tu independencia 100%. Solo te brindamos la infraestructura."

2. **On Growth:** "Nuestros abogados crecen un promedio de 40% en clientes en el primer año."

3. **On Support:** "Tendrás acceso a capacitación, marketing y soporte administrativo."

4. **On Community:** "Serás parte de una comunidad de profesionales que colaboran y aprenden juntos."

---

## Exit Conditions

- [ ] Professional understands the opportunity
- [ ] Concerns addressed
- [ ] Next steps scheduled
- [ ] Agreement initiated or declined
