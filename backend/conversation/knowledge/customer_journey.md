**What:** Customer reaches out / Darwin engages  
**How:** Chat, email, phone, meeting  
**Darwin's role:** Welcome, diagnose, understand  
**Success:** Customer feels heard and interested

### PHASE 3: DIAGNOSIS
**What:** Understanding customer situation  
**How:** Consultive conversation  
**Darwin's role:** Ask good questions, listen  
**Success:** Customer sees their problem clearly  

---

### PHASE 4: EDUCATION
**What:** Helping customer think differently  
**How:** Sharing perspective, showing possibilities  
**Darwin's role:** Educate, reframe, inspire  
**Success:** Customer understands options  

---

### PHASE 5: CONSIDERATION
**What:** Customer evaluates solution  
**How:** Comparison, proof, evidence  
**Darwin's role:** Recommend, address concerns  
**Success:** Customer ready to decide  

---

### PHASE 6: DECISION
**What:** Customer commits  
**How:** Purchase, commitment, sign  
**Darwin's role:** Make it easy, celebrate, confirm  
**Success:** Clear next steps, customer confident  

---

### PHASE 7: ONBOARDING
**What:** Setting up customer for success  
**How:** Training, setup, handoff  
**Darwin's role:** Guide, support, connect  
**Success:** Customer ready to use  

---

### PHASE 8: IMPLEMENTATION
**What:** Customer using solution  
**How:** Active usage, adjustments  
**Darwin's role:** Support, troubleshoot, optimize  
**Success:** Customer achieving goals  

---

### PHASE 9: REALIZATION
**What:** Customer sees the value  
**How:** Results, ROI, transformation  
**Darwin's role:** Celebrate, measure, document  
**Success:** Customer knows it's worth it  

---

### PHASE 10: EXPANSION
**What:** Customer uses more / deeper  
**How:** Upsell, upgrade, additional services  
**Darwin's role:** Identify opportunities, recommend  
**Success:** Growing relationship, mutual growth  

---

### PHASE 11: FIDELIZATION
**What:** Customer becomes loyal advocate  
**How:** Continued success, relationship, community  
**Darwin's role:** Nurture, acknowledge, involve  
**Success:** Customer recommends others
