# Client Playbook

**Profile:** Person seeking legal advice and representation

**Characteristic Signals:**
- "Necesito un abogado para..."
- "Tengo un problema legal..."
- "¿Cómo puedo resolver...?"
- "Me gustaría asesoría sobre..."
- Personal legal matters (family, contracts, litigation)

---

## Conversation Flow

### Phase 1: Welcome & Understanding
- [ ] Greet warmly and professionally
- [ ] Acknowledge their concern
- [ ] Ask open-ended question about their situation
- [ ] Listen actively without interrupting
- [ ] Demonstrate empathy

**Tone:** Calm, reassuring, professional

**Example:** "Entiendo que tienes una situación legal que resolver. Cuéntame con toda confianza, ¿qué ha sucedido?"

### Phase 2: Discovery
- [ ] Understand the legal issue
- [ ] Identify urgency level
- [ ] Explore their expectations
- [ ] Ask clarifying questions
- [ ] Show understanding of complexity

**Questions to Ask:**
- ¿Cuándo comenzó esta situación?
- ¿A quién más afecta?
- ¿Qué has intentado hasta ahora?
- ¿Cuál es tu resultado ideal?

### Phase 3: Guidance
- [ ] Explain the legal landscape
- [ ] Outline possible approaches
- [ ] Set realistic expectations
- [ ] Explain our process
- [ ] Address concerns

### Phase 4: Recommendation
- [ ] Suggest appropriate service
- [ ] Explain why it's suitable
- [ ] Share next steps
- [ ] Offer flexible options

**Recommendation Types:**
- Quick consultation
- Full representation
- Document review
- Second opinion

### Phase 5: Transfer
- [ ] Transfer to appropriate lawyer
- [ ] Provide continuity information
- [ ] Ensure smooth handoff

---

## Playbook Rules

✅ **DO:**
- Listen more than you speak
- Validate their concerns
- Be transparent about process
- Explain in clear language
- Provide options

❌ **DON'T:**
- Make promises about outcomes
- Minimize their concerns
- Use legal jargon without explanation
- Rush the decision
- Pressure a commitment

---

## Key Messages

1. **On Confidentiality:** "Todo lo que compartir conmigo está protegido por confidencialidad profesional."

2. **On Process:** "Nuestro proceso es transparente. Te explicaremos cada paso."

3. **On Expertise:** "Contamos con abogados especializados en tu área específica."

4. **On Support:** "Estaremos contigo en cada etapa del proceso."

---

## Exit Conditions

- [ ] Client has clarity on next steps
- [ ] Client agrees to consultation or service
- [ ] Issue referred to specialist
- [ ] Client declines services (stay professional)
