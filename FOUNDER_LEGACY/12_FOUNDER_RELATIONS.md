## LEVEL 3: DARWIN CORE → KNOWLEDGE LIBRARY

### How Darwin Uses Knowledge

Darwin consults the Knowledge Library for:

**Operational Information**
- Products, pricing, plans, countries, FAQ
- When: Customer asks specific questions
- Purpose: Accurate, up-to-date information

**Business Rules**
- Policies, legal, privacy, security, commercial
- When: Customer situation has compliance implications
- Purpose: Safe, legal interactions

**Conversational Approaches**
- Playbooks, sales flows, profile-specific guidance
- When: Routing to appropriate conversation pattern
- Purpose: Effective customer guidance

## LEVEL 4: KNOWLEDGE LIBRARY → PLAYBOOKS

### How Knowledge Becomes Actions

**Founder Philosophy** (from Business_Philosophy.md)
↓
**Master Book Principles** (from Chapter 4)
↓
**Knowledge Library** (operational implementation)
↓
**Playbooks** (how to approach specific situations)

Example:
- Founder believes: "Trust is earned, not given"
- Master Book states: "We build relationships, not transactions"
- Knowledge includes: Sales objections framework
- Playbook provides: Client discovery flow that builds trust through listening

---

## LEVEL 5: PLAYBOOKS → AGENTS & CONVERSATIONS

### How Guidance Becomes Interactions

**Commercial Playbook** (how to approach prospects)
→ **CommercialAgent** (who decides on guidance)
→ **ConversationRouter** (who routes to right channel)
→ **Actual Customer Conversation** (where value is delivered)

At every step, founder philosophy is preserved:
- Agent decides *how* to approach (from philosophy)
- Router decides *which* playbook applies (from playbook)
- Conversation executes with integrity (from master book principles)

---

## LEVEL 6: CONVERSATIONS → CUSTOMER EXPERIENCE

### The Final Output

Every customer conversation is shaped by:

1. **Master Book principles** - What matters
2. **Founder philosophy** - How to approach
3. **Darwin core** - How to think
4. **Knowledge** - What to say
5. **Playbook** - Conversation flow
6. **Agent** - Decision-making

Result: **A customer experiences the founder's values through the conversation**

## VERTICAL SCALING THROUGH FOUNDER LEGACY

### How New Verticals Use Founder Legacy

**Phase 1: Foundation**
1. Read Master Book (especially Chapters 1, 2, 3, 4)
2. Read Founder Legacy (especially 01, 02, 07)
3. Adapt for new vertical context

**Phase 2: Operation**
1. Create vertical-specific Knowledge Library
2. Create vertical-specific Playbooks
3. Adapt Product Philosophy for vertical

**Phase 3: Growth**
1. New vertical develops its own Customer Observations
2. New vertical learns from Mistakes and Lessons
3. New vertical contributes back to Master Book evolution

Result: **All verticals maintain founder DNA while adapting to context**
