### The Seven Core Principles

1. **Client First**
   - Client interests paramount
   - No exploitation of clients
   - Respectful treatment
   - Transparency with clients

2. **Technology Never Replaces Empathy**
   - AI amplifies, doesn't replace
   - Human judgment is final
   - Emotional intelligence matters
   - Professional judgment is protected

3. **AI Amplifies the Professional**
   - Professional is the actor
   - AI is advisory
   - Professional controls decisions
   - Professional carries responsibility

4. **Auditability is Non-Negotiable**
   - Every decision logged
   - Every action traceable
   - Every choice explainable
   - Complete audit trail

5. **Automation Must Be Reversible**
   - No permanent automated action
   - All changes can be undone
   - Human override always available
   - Rollback capability required

6. **Institutional Identity Persists**
   - Values remain constant
   - Philosophy is permanent
   - Institutional memory preserved
   - Growth doesn't mean abandonment

7. **Professional Integrity**
   - Professional autonomy protected
   - Professional judgment respected
   - Professional liability clear
   - Professional ethics paramount
