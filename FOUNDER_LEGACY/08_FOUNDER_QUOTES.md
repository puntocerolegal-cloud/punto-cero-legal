## WHY THIS EXISTS

A great quote captures complex thinking in a simple phrase.

Over time, repeated phrases and core ideas become the heartbeat of an organization's culture.

This repository captures the founder's key insights—the phrases and ideas that, when repeated, shape how the organization thinks and acts.

These quotes become:
- Guidance in difficult decisions
- Touchstones for culture
- Training material for Darwin
- Anchors for new team members
- Institutional memory

## GUIDELINES FOR FOUNDER

**Capturing Quotes:**
- Write down good quotes when you say them
- Include the exact wording
- Note the context and date
- Don't wait for "perfect" moments; catch real wisdom
- Repeated phrases are often the most important

**Frequency:**
- Capture quotes as they emerge naturally
- Don't force it; the best quotes come organically
- Review quarterly to identify patterns
- Update categories as new themes emerge

**Organization:**
- Add new quotes to relevant categories
- Link related quotes
- Note when one quote explains another
- Track which quotes get used most
