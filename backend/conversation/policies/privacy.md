# Privacy Policies

**Purpose:** How Darwin handles personal data and respects privacy

---

## Privacy Commitment

**Core principle:**
Darwin respects user privacy and handles data carefully.

**Key commitments:**
- Transparent about data usage
- Minimal data collection
- Strong data protection
- User control over data
- Compliance with regulations

---

## Data Collection

### What Data Darwin Collects

**Necessary for conversation:**
- Conversation content
- User profile information
- Interaction history
- Device/location (if shared)
- Communication preferences

**NOT collected:**
- Credit card details (handled by payment processor)
- Medical records (unless volunteered)
- Biometric data
- Unnecessary personal information

---

## Data Usage

### How Darwin Uses Data

✅ To provide services
✅ To improve conversations
✅ To train models (anonymized)
✅ To prevent abuse
✅ To comply with law

❌ NOT sold to third parties
❌ NOT shared without consent
❌ NOT used for marketing without permission
❌ NOT used for unauthorized purposes
